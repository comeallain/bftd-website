    About Us

    The Company

    Every great story deserves to be told. We find the ones that have been buried, forgotten, or silenced — and bring them back from the dead.

    — Back From The Dead Productions

    Reviving Cinema from Los Angeles

    Back From The Dead Productions is a creatively driven, global production company headquartered in Los Angeles. We specialise in crafting groundbreaking movies and documentaries rooted in real events — stories that matter, stories that challenge, stories the world needs to hear.

    Founded by filmmaker Richie Smyth, BFTD was born from a singular conviction: the most powerful stories are the ones that actually happened. From the front lines of geopolitical conflict to the corridors of corporate power, we go where the truth takes us.

    Our approach is simple: pair fearless investigative storytelling with world-class cinematic craft. No compromises on substance. No compromises on style.

    Truth First

    Every project begins with rigorous research and real-world immersion. We don't invent stories — we uncover them.

    Cinematic Craft

    True stories deserve blockbuster production value. We bring Hollywood craft to stories that Hollywood overlooks.

    Global Reach

    Our stories cross borders. From the Middle East to the halls of international finance, we produce content that resonates worldwide.

    The Filmmaker

    Richie Smyth

    Richie Smyth

    Founder · Director · Writer · Producer

    Richie Smyth is the creative force behind Back From The Dead Productions. A filmmaker with an instinct for stories at the intersection of truth and cinema, Richie has spent over a decade developing and producing content that pushes boundaries.

    His work spans feature films, documentary series, music videos and branded content — but his passion lies in long-form narrative rooted in real events. With projects currently in development including Zarqawi and The Dechert Incident, Richie is building a slate that proves truth is always stranger than fiction.
