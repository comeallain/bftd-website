About Us

The Company

Every great story deserves to be told. We find the ones that have been buried, forgotten, or silenced and bring them back from the dead.

Back From The Dead Productions

Reviving Cinema from Los Angeles to Dublin

Back From The Dead Productions is a creatively driven, global production company based in Los Angeles and Dublin. We specialise in crafting groundbreaking movies and documentaries rooted in real events, stories that matter, stories that challenge, stories the world needs to hear.

Founded by Filmmaker Richie Smyth and Producer Kevin O'Meallain, Back From The Dead Productions was born from a singular conviction: the most powerful stories are the ones that actually happened. From the front lines of geopolitical conflict to the corridors of corporate power, we go where the truth takes us.

Our approach is simple: pair fearless investigative storytelling with world-class cinematic craft. No compromises on substance. No compromises on style.

The Filmmaker

Richie Smyth

Richie Smyth

Founder · Director · Writer · Producer

Richie Smyth is an internationally acclaimed, award-winning filmmaker and the creative force behind Back From The Dead Productions. He is best known for directing The Siege of Jadotville, a geo-political war thriller starring Jamie Dornan, Mark Strong, and Guillaume Canet, which became one of Netflix's first original feature films and won three Irish Film & Television Awards, including Best Director.

Richie's career spans two decades of work across feature films, music videos for U2, The Verve, Bon Jovi, and Melissa Etheridge, and globally recognised brand campaigns for Nike, Guinness, Pepsi, and PETA. His work is recognised for its edgy photographic style, its beauty and humanity. Based between Los Angeles and Dublin, Richie is currently developing Fighting Charity and The Dechert Incident, proving that truth is always stranger than fiction.
